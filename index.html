Good-to-knows

Parking

Use SpotHero the night before for downtown parking. Park once and walk when possible.

Cruise tickets

Book the Wendella 4:00 PM cruise ahead of time. Arrive about 30 minutes early.

Beach day

Bring sunscreen, towels, water, and shade. Freeze a few water bottles overnight.

Fireworks

For easier parking, watch from Milton Lee Olive Park or near Ohio Street Beach instead of parking at Navy Pier.
